ALTITUDE is an annual electronic dance music festival held all over the world, with its grand south-east Asian flagship event holding in Tasik Villa International Resort, Lukut, Port Dickson, Malaysia. The event primarily features top and renowned electronic dance producers and DJs from around the globe with various sounds from beyond the stratosphere.

ALTITUDE was created with the purpose of utilizing music and art to inspire individuals. In addition to the music, individuals can experience the three-dimensional superstructures, colorful, glow-in-the-dark environments, and all manner of LED-infused flora and fauna. Part of the unforgettable experience will be the interactive art installations scattered throughout ALTITUDE and the thrilling, as well as heart-racing neon-lit rollercoaster rides coupled with a host of intense musical shows.

The goal is ultimately for ALTITUDE to be a place where thousands of people of various nationalities can connect, on and off the dance floor. This superpacked one-day event welcomes everyone, not discriminating against shape, size, and color. Be ready for an unforgettable surprise.
