FAQs

ALTITUDE Music Festival will be held on August 24, 2019 from 3:00PM until 3:00AM.

Our festival site is located on the beautiful open space of the Tasik Villa International Resort, Port Dickson, Malaysia. It is only 98 kilometers from Kuala Lumpur and about 50 kilometers from Kuala Lumpur International Airport by road. We have attached a map link below to help attendees locate the festival venue.

After purchase of tickets, an electronically generated e-mail will be sent out with ticket details and event information. Please print out your e-tickets and bring them to the event venue. RFID wristbands will be given in order to gain access to the event. Also, wristbands are not transferrable, so any persons with damaged wristbands will be denied access into event grounds. There will be a RM20.00 fee to reprint tickets for attendees who fail to present the e-tickets on the event day. There is strictly no replacement for missing tickets and cancellations.

Festival goers can access the location by car or Grab. People who wish to use Grab will need the Grab app on their mobile phones. Alternative means of transportaion is by bus from the TBS (Terminal Bersepadu Selatan) to Seremban, thereafter another bus to Port Dickson. There is no direct buses from Kuala Lumpur to Port Dickson. Also this will mean lots of traffic. Lots of it during peak periods. So please, consider ridesharing – it helps reduce traffic, saves money on gas, and the earth will love you.

The organizers of ALTITUDE have made arrangements for free shuttles from Kuala Lumpur to Tasik Villa International Resort, Port Dickson. Registration procedures for this service will be on our website.

For those who wish to spend the night in Port Dickson, there are numerous resorts around and also some cheap and affordable hotels in the city.

The entire event is subjected to attendees above the age of 18.

There will be multiple vending machines located at the festival venue to provide attendees with beverages, soft drinks, water and other snacks. Please note that this is not free and you will need your RFID wristbands to make purchases so please top up at the entrace to the event. Also, we have made arrangements with local food courts to mount stalls at the event venue to provide food and drinks to the public. This can be paid only with cash.

Scattered around the festival ground will be our customized upside down photo booths to add a twist and capture your wildest experiences. Drop by with friends and loved ones and flick up. Remember you'll need your RFID wristbands to make use of these services.

As your security and the safety of your items is our main concern, we will be providing temporary lockers at the event site for people who need to leave some of their valuable items before proceeding to the main stage. Please note that ALTITUDE is not responsible for any loss of items except for the ones stored in the lockers provided and to that point, please make sure you're in possession of your locker keys at all times.

We at ALTITUDE are committed to delivering nothing but the best. Details of lineups and performing acts will be sent out in e-mails as well as will be updated on the event site. Get ready for an exciting line-up of your top international and local performers on stage and be rest assured, we have a lot of surprises in store for this so stay tuned in.

12 hours of non-stop heart-racing electronic dance, trance, house and other genres of music from our list of incredible international and local DJs.

We strongly prohibit entry into event venue with any kind of weapons, sharp objects, explosives, fireworks, flammable liquids, illegal substances, drugs, laser pens, pets (no matter how small). Any violations of the above rules or related at the event will lead to an automatic expulsion from festival grounds without any refunds. We strongly encourage good behaviour and respect for one another during the course of the event.

Lose something at the festival? Head to the Info Booth at event site to see if your item has been recovered. If the Info Booth doesn’t have it you shouldn’t give up yet! Head on over to altitudemusicfestival.com/lost to submit a claim. If a fellow festival attendee turns it in we’ll be able to match it up with you.
